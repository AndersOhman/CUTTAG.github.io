2. Peak Calling Statistics

MACS2 2.1.2 was used to call peaks for each sample in group Fetal-Dual-2m1. Called peaks were merged within each group and peaks called in at least 2 samples were kept for downstream analysis.

3.2 Peak annotation by genomic feature

Peaks were annotated based on the genomic features they overlap. When annotations overlap assignment priorities are: Promoter > 5’UTR > 3’UTR > Exon > Intron > Downstream > Intergenic.
